The main goal of this sport is to maneuver and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters, often times taking you over rocks and drops, without capsizing the raft

History

In the late 1950s the lodge began offering float trips. they used surplus war rafts that were 8 ft wide by 27 ft long. Less than 500 people went that year but the demand started increasing. It wasn ºt until the 1960s and 70s that this whitewater thrills Company started forming.
